Our Mission

Create and develop a sustainable esports organization model and be the home of rising champions.


Create sustainable structures and business concepts in esports and gaming industry to develop a healthy ecosystem
for the organization, players, partners, sponsors and investors to thrive. Through our vast and ever-growing
international network, we seek to build the framework for establishing, developing, building and pushing the next
generation of esport talents. We do this by following young talents through their career journey assisting them
through the different tiers of esport, where we guide and develop them in all personal areas to reach the best
possible performance framework.


We create accessible, fine-tuned games that can succeed commercially with a global audience.
We actively support charitable initiatives with a particular focus on helping children. And we step
up in times of need to assist local, national and international causes.


We do our best to create, innovate and inspire our fans, players, investors and sponsors.
